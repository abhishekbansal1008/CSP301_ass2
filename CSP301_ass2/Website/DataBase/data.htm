**MP Rating :

Calculated by taking the weighted mean of each individual ratings of education, attendance, debates, questions raised and bills passed by the MP on scale of 5. The weights are selected on the relevance of the fields in the MPs activity in the Parliament.
Formula used: (0.8(education) + 1.2(debates) + 1.4(bills) + 1.1(questions) + 1.5(attendance))/6
Each individual rating is decided by calculating its deviation from the mean
