• Hypothesis 1: More experienced MPs maintain better attendance.


    Consider two sample sets:
    Sample-1 : Attendance of MPs with age less than the average age
    Sample-2 : Attendance of MPs with age more than the average age
    Null Hypothesis, H0 : μ1 = μ2
    Alternate Hypothesis, HA : μ1 > μ2

    AFTER WELCH T-TEST
    T-value, t = -3.1814299415896183
    Degree of Freedom, df = 487
    Using T-table (α(1 tail) = 0.05), since |t| > tcritical, therefore, H0 can be rejected with 95% confidence level.
    Also, since t is negative, therefore, HA is also rejected.

    Thus, Hypothesis-1 can be accepted with 95% confidence level.
